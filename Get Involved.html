Why It Is Important to Donate

Donations help orphanages pay for kids’ education by covering things like school fees, books, and uniforms. They can also help build classrooms, hire teachers, and get computers or other supplies. With donations, kids can go to school, join activities like art or sports, and even learn job skills when they’re older. This gives them a chance to have a better future and live a good life.
