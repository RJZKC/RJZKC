Teaching Financial Literacy at Sta. Maria de Mattias Center Orphanage

We will teach the orphans at Sta. Maria de Mattias Center Orphanage about finances or budgeting. We will also give them homework assignments so we can see if they have learned anything. We will also give them books about finances

Materials

  • Book
    • About budgeting and Finance
  • Assignmentns
    • We can create assignments for them to answer after the presentation
  • Slideshow Presentasion in Laptop
    • There is a group in the class that can do the presentation we are going to teach

The materials can be purchased at national bookstores or online shops like Lazada. The laptop can be borrowed from a classmate.
