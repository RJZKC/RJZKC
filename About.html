The Project

This project is to teach the orphans about financial managing and budgeting. It will prepare them for their future self where in the real world, budgeting is a very important skill in life we need to master. With budgeting, we are able to save money and prioritize our needs before our wants.

Goals

1. Their budgeting can be enhanced for when they get older

2. To be responsible about how they spend their money

3. Use the information learned in their daily lives

4. To be able to know the true value of money
